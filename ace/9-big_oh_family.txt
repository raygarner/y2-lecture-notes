big-Omega definition:
    same as big-Oh but with 'f(n)>=c*g(n)' instead of 'f(n)<=c*g(n)'
    reflexive
    not symmetric
    transitive

big-Theta
    like big-Oh but there must be c', c'', n0 such that:
        f(n)<=c'*g(n) and
        f(n)>=c''*g(n)
    reflexive
    transitive
    IS symmetric
    behaves like equality
